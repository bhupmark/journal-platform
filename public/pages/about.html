About the Journal

International Research Journal of Modernization in Engineering, Technology and Science

Journal Overview

The International Research Journal of Modernization in Engineering, Technology and Science (IRJMETS) is a peer-reviewed, open-access international journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research papers in the fields of Engineering, Science, Technology, and allied disciplines.

The journal aims to provide a global platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to share innovative ideas, experimental results, and emerging technologies.
