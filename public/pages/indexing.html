CrossRef / DOI

Every published article receives a Digital Object Identifier (DOI) through CrossRef. DOIs ensure permanent, resolvable links (e.g. https://doi.org/10.1234/jor.2024.12.3.001). Please cite articles using their DOI for maximum reliability.
