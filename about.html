About the Journal

International Research Journal of Modernization in Engineering, Technology and Science

Journal Overview

The International Research Journal of Modernization in Engineering, Technology and Science (IRJMETS) is a peer-reviewed, open-access international journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research papers in the fields of Engineering, Science, Technology, and allied disciplines.

The journal aims to provide a global platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to share innovative ideas, experimental results, and emerging technologies.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized research journal that promotes innovation, quality research, and technological advancement for societal development.

Our Mission

  • Encourage original and innovative research
  • Maintain high ethical publication standards
  • Provide rapid and transparent peer review
  • Support interdisciplinary research

Scope of the Journal

The journal accepts research papers, review papers, and case studies in areas including but not limited to:

Engineering Computer Science Artificial Intelligence Data Science Electronics Mechanical Engineering Civil Engineering Applied Sciences

Publication Details

  • Journal Type: Peer-Reviewed, Open Access
  • Frequency: Monthly
  • Language: English
  • Review Process: Double-Blind Peer Review
