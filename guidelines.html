Guidelines

Author Guidelines

Manuscripts must be original, not published elsewhere, and not under consideration by another journal. Use the journal template and follow the structure: Title, Abstract (150–250 words), Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References. References should follow APA or IEEE style as applicable to the discipline. Figures and tables must be numbered and cited in the text.

Submit in Word (.docx) or LaTeX. Maximum length for research articles is typically 8,000 words excluding references; for short communications, 3,000 words. Ensure all authors have agreed to submission and that corresponding author contact details are correct.

Review Process

Submissions are first checked for completeness and scope. Suitable manuscripts are sent for double-blind peer review to at least two reviewers. You will receive a decision: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject. Revised manuscripts should be resubmitted with a response letter. We aim for first decision within 6–8 weeks. Accepted papers undergo copyediting and typesetting before publication.

Publication Charges

To support open access, we apply an Article Processing Charge (APC) after acceptance. Waivers or discounts are available for authors from low- and middle-income countries or in cases of genuine financial hardship. Contact the editorial office for details.

  • Research article: $XXX (waiver available)
  • Short communication: $XXX
  • No charge for rejected or withdrawn submissions.

Withdrawal Policy

Authors may withdraw a submission before it is sent for review by contacting the editorial office. Once under review, withdrawal requests are considered on a case-by-case basis. Withdrawal after acceptance may incur a fee and require a formal letter. Unethical withdrawal (e.g., after acceptance to submit elsewhere) may result in sanctions.
